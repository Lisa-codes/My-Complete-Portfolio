Padel Tennis Mobile App

The Padel Tennis mobile app showcases the Village Market padel tennis game, providing users with a seamless way to book and pay for their sessions directly through the app. Featuring an integrated QR code system, users can effortlessly add friends and enjoy the game together. This app streamlines the booking process, enhancing the overall user experience and making it easier to enjoy padel tennis at the Village Market.

Bowling Queue System

I created an internal bowling queue system to manage high-traffic days at a the Village Market mall's bowling alley. This system simplifies the booking process, making it easier for staff to handle large client volumes. Key features include "notified/pending" status bars to track ticket information and clickable lane cards for editing lane occupancy. The design adheres to WCAG guidelines, ensuring consistency and accessibility.
